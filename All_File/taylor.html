Who is Taylor Swift?

Taylor Alison Swift, born December 13, 1989 is a Grammy Award-winning singer-songwriter. She began making a splash in the country music world in 2006 and has risen to become one of the most popular artists of all time. She began writing her own songs and learning guitar at 12 years old. By the age of 16, she has already earned much recognition as a country music singer. In 2010, her early hits "Love Story" and "You Belong With Me" in the album Fearless helped her win her first Grammy for Album of the Year. Later on, she began to step further away from her country music roots, releasing the album "1989" with "Shake It Off" making it to the top of the pop charts in October 2014. Her most recent albums include "Folklore" and "Evermore" which was released in 2020 also gained immense commercial success. Taylor continues to leave her marks by re-recording her albums in an effort to reclaim her master records that were sold by her old label, Big Machine Records. Today, Swift has re-recorded Fearless and Red as "Fearless (Taylor's Version) and "Red (Taylor's Version) to reclaim the work she was never given the chance to own.
