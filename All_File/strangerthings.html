About Stranger Things

Stranger Things is an American sci-fi television series created by the Duffer Brothers. The show was first released on July 15, 2016 as a Netflix original series. The show is set in Hawkins, Indiana, a fictional town in the mid 1980s. The first season took place in November 1983. The numerous characters separately investigated the disappearance of Will Byers. Season 2 and 3 expand the narrative scope and explore the aftermath of the events of November 1983. The fourth season, released on May 27th, 2022 further expand the show's scope where major events now take place outside of Hawkins for the first time. Yet, every season shares the similarity for containing numerous supernatural events occuring around Hawkins, especially around a connection to a hostile alternate reality called the Upside Down. Season 5 is in the work as numerous fans await the ending of one of the most sucessful Netflix Series ever.
